How to Update Pawfit GPS Map?

Pawfit GPS trackers help you monitor your pet’s location, activity, and safety in real time. To ensure peak accuracy and performance, it's important to regularly update the device’s maps and firmware. This guide walks you through the complete update process.

Why Keep Your Pawfit GPS Updated?

  • Enhances tracking precision
  • Improves battery and system performance
  • Adds new features and geofencing updates
  • Fixes bugs or security vulnerabilities

What You'll Need

  • Pawfit GPS Tracker with at least 50% battery
  • Smartphone with the Pawfit app installed
  • Active Bluetooth and Wi-Fi or mobile data
  • Your Pawfit login account

Step-by-Step Map & Firmware Update

1. Open the Pawfit App

Launch the app and log in to your account. If you forgot your credentials, recover them from the Pawfit login page.

2. Go to Device Settings

Select your pet’s tracker and open the Device Settings section.

3. Check for Updates

Tap on Firmware Update. If new GPS maps or software are available, you’ll be prompted to download them.

4. Start the Update

Ensure stable connection and follow on-screen instructions. The update may take 5–10 minutes.

5. Confirm Completion

After the update, confirm the version change by checking the firmware or map version inside the app.

Important: Don’t disconnect the device or close the app during updates. It could lead to corrupted firmware.

Having Trouble?

Try the following if your update doesn't go smoothly:

  • Restart the Pawfit device and your phone
  • Ensure Bluetooth and location services are enabled
  • Make sure your app is updated to the latest version
  • Contact Pawfit Support for help

Update Frequency

Check for updates every month to ensure smooth tracking, especially if you're using features like live tracking, health monitoring, or geofencing.

Conclusion

Keeping your Pawfit GPS tracker updated helps ensure your pet’s safety, accurate tracking, and optimal performance. By following the steps above, you can maintain the device’s firmware and maps with ease. Stay up to date—because your pet deserves the best protection.
